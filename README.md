## 📖 THE LORE: SDG 13 CLIMATE ACTION
The world has reached a tipping point. Global temperatures have destabilized the surface, forcing humanity to look to the skies. You are piloting an experimental high-altitude Exo-Suit designed to collect atmospheric data and restore the planet's balance. 

Every jump drains your suit's finite energy reserves. To survive, you must master kinetic momentum, upgrade your tech with renewable energy modules, and answer critical **Climate Action Quizzes** when your system fails. Education is your ultimate survival tool. If you don't know how to save the planet in the terminal, you won't survive the climb.

## 🧠 THE ENGINEERING MASTERCLASS
This project pushes the limits of vanilla web technologies, operating entirely without heavy external game engines.

* **Zero-Error Checkpoint Math:** A custom algorithmic loop evaluates `Math.floor(alt / 500)` against previous platform states, guaranteeing a Blue Platform and 🚩 Flag generation at exactly every 500m boundary.
* **Web Audio API Synthesizer:** No external MP3s are used for sound effects. Every jump, coin, and warning klaxon is synthesized in real-time using generated sine, square, and sawtooth waves.
* **GAS Database Architecture:** Utilizing Google Apps Script as a serverless backend to handle cross-device highscore validation, credit economy tracking, and inventory synchronization via a 3-second heartbeat pulse.
